dabeli

Dabeli, kutchi dabeli or double roti is a popular snack food of India, originating in the Kutch or Kachchh region of Gujarat. vada pav

Vada pav, alternatively spelt wada pao, is a vegetarian fast food dish native to the state of Maharashtra. The dish consists of a deep fried potato dumpling placed inside a bread bun sliced almost in half through the middle. It is generally accompanied with one or more chutneys and a green chili pepper. Wikipedia Serving temperature: Hot or Room Temperature Place of origin: India Invented: 1966 Region or state: Maharashtra Alternative names: vada pao, wada pav, wada pao, pao vada, pav vada, pao wada, pav wada, batata wada pav

pav bhaji

Pav bhaji is a fast food dish from India consisting of a thick vegetable curry served with a soft bread roll. Its origins are in the state of Maharashtra. Wikipedia Region or state: Maharashtra Main ingredients: Bread, Potato, Garlic, Tomato, Onions, Mixed vegetables
